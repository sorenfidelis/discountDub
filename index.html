Welcome to Discount Duplication

Discount Duplication has been in business since 1984. Conveniently located in the upcoming part of old Birmingham – Avondale.

Visit our studio Monday – Friday 9am - 5pm & Saturday 10am - 4pm.

at: 212 41st Street South Birmingham Alabama 35222.

 

We provide high quality fast and affordable service meeting your every need in transferring, converting, enhancing and preserving your treasured memories. In our more than 3000 sq feet studio we have state of the art equipment allowing us to transfer video tapes such as: VHS, SVHS, Video 8, HI 8, Digital 8, Mini DV, DVCAM, Foreign PAL and SECAM tapes. We also have the latest in HD Film Scanning Technology, transferring old films 8mm, Super 8, 16mm to any format.

For audio fans trying to listen to good oldies using modern media, we can convert: cassette tapes, vinyl, reel to reel to CD, wav or MP3 files.

We also have full service production equipment allowing us to duplicate small or large DVD or CD duplication orders very quickly. We can take care of all your packaging, printing and duplicating needs.
